Cyberpunk Button
-------------------------

A sleek, futuristic interactive button designed for fun, websites, apps, or commercial use.
Features glowing neon effects, stardust particle bursts, glitch visuals, and immersive sound effect.

Developed by: ShinEcliptix
GitHub: https://github.com/ShinEcliptix

Use Cases:
- Websites
- Games
- Personal projects
- Commercial products
- UI demos
- And whatever you want

Audio:
- Pressed sound (local file 'button_sound.mp3' required)
